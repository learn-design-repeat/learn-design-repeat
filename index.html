Oreo boost ice-cream

No churning. Crush. Whip. Mix. Freeze

Img source: flickr.com

Ingredients:

  • Oreo cookies - 3 small packets
  • Milkmaid - 200ml
  • Boost - 2tsp
  • Heavy cream(Cold) - 500ml

Directions:

  1. Crush the Oreo cookies nicely.
  2. Place cold heavy cream in a large bowl, whip to stiff peaks.
  3. Add sweetened condensed milk and beat on low speed until incorporated, add crushed cookies and boost,then fold.
  4. Transfer into a container/dish, top with 3-4 extra crushed cookies and freeze for least 6 hours.
